Upload App Notes



Steps To Upload an Android App in Google Play Store:

#1
Get a GooglePlay Console Account.
 1.1 Sign-In to Play Console
 1.2 Fill in the required details.
 1.3 Need to pay 25$ to open an account. Please Make sure that you have a Debit/Credit Card that supports VISA or MASTERCARD payments.

#2
After Step:1, Click on Create App button.

#3
Enter App details: App name, Default language, Is this an App or game, Is this a free one or Paid one, etc
And Click on Submit button.

#4
Now you'll be able to see the Dashboard - Get started setting up your app.
 You should Provide information about your app and set up your Store Listing.
 Click on View Tasks.

 Objective type questions will be asked in few sections, Based on your App type, Select the option for
  App access - based on login credentials, memberships, location, etc
  Ads - Does your app contain ads?
  Content ratings - What Category it falls to? etc
  Target audience and content - Who can see this?
  News apps - is this a news app?
  Store settings - App Category listing
  Main store listing - Add details about your app: Description, Images Screenshots, etc

#5
If you are interested, You can Release your app early for internal testing without review.

#6
If you are interested, Test your app with a larger group of testers that you control. Let anyone sign up to test your app on Google Play
and/or Build excitement for your app with pre-registration

#7
Once all the above steps are done, complete the following section - Publish your app on Google Play
 Select Countries - Your app can be shown in selected/all Countries.

#8
We need to upload the APK now, so Click on Create New Release Button.
 8.1 Accept the App Signing Terms and Conditions.
 8.2 Now we need to Drop Android App Bundles (.aab) or upload the APK.


#9
To upload the APK, the APK has to be Signed APK. Please follow the steps to generate the Signed APK.
 * Go to your Android Studio.
 * Click on Build -> Click on Generate Signed Bundle / APK option.
  If you are having a Flutter App, try this Go to Tools-> Flutter -> Open for editing in android studio -> New Window. The options start showing up in the Build menu.
 * Select Android App Bundle and Click Next.
 * If you have a Key Store file already, then just choose the existing file. Or else click on Create New button.
  (DO NOT LOSE THIS FILE ONCE AFTER CREATION, OR ELSE YOU WON'T BE ABLE TO UPDATE APP WITHOUT THIS KEY)
 * Start filing up the sections and click on Next.
 * Select Release Build Variant and Click on Finish.
 * This will generate the *.aab APK Bundle. You need to upload this *.aab file. Add Version Name and Release Notes.

#10
Click on Review Release.

#11
Click on Start Roll-out Button.

CHEERS!!!! YOU HAVE UPLOADED YOUR NEW APP AND LET'S JUST WAIT FOR THE REVIEW TO HAPPEN.
